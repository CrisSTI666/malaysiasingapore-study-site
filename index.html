Why Choose Singapore?

  • Close to Malaysia – just a short flight or bus ride away
  • English-speaking environment
  • World-class universities and private institutions
  • High graduate employability and safe city life

Top Private Universities for Malaysian Students

  • Kaplan Singapore – Business, IT, Psychology
  • James Cook University (JCU) – Tourism, Psychology, Environmental Science
  • PSB Academy – Engineering, Life Sciences, Accounting

How to Apply

  1. Choose your course and institution
  2. Prepare required documents (SPM/UEC results, passport copy, English test if any)
  3. Submit your application through us (free service)
  4. Receive offer letter and proceed with student pass application

Student Visa Information

Malaysian students require a Student Pass approved by Singapore's ICA. Processing usually takes 2–4 weeks. We assist all applicants with visa procedures and documentation support.
